Trackgenix

Trackgenix is an incredible tool that will help your team thrive.

Trackgenix is a time tracking software that makes it easy to track the hours worked by every employee on every project and work team. By using our time tracker, you can impress your clients with honest data and easily evaluate future projects.

Functionalities

Timesheet

An intuitive, lightweight time tracking experience that's easy to adopt and use every day.

Reports

A large selection of visual reports keeps projects running smoothly and supporting your team.

Resources Management

Manage your resources effectively and efficiently as well as track internal costs and review past project data to inform future project scope and estimates.

Roles

Timetracker allows you to define the role of each employee in each project and with their own rate in an easy and fast way.

¿Why do you use Trackgenix?

Productivity Booster

Increase your team's productivity by knowing how many hours employees can work each week or on each project.

Work Tracking

Track the progress of your work teams as they go. Spot problems early and fight scope creep with easy-to-read visual data.

Leadership and team management

Improve your leadership. See who's overworked and who's underutilized at a glance with capacity reports.

Decision making

Enhance your decisions based on data collected from previous projects.
